---
title: No COA Received With Order
description: What to do if you haven’t received your certificate of analysis.
---

## Issue

- Order arrived but no COA included
- You need COA for verification

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check email inbox">
    COA is sent automatically to your order email—look for a message from `support@claritylabsusa.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check spam/junk folder">
    Sometimes emails are filtered by mistake.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request COA resending">
    Email your order number to `support@claritylabsusa.com` and request another copy of your COA.
  </Step>
</Steps>
