## Placing an Order

1. Visit `https://claritylabsusa.com`
2. Browse by peptide or category
3. Click the item you want
4. Choose quantity and add to cart
5. Go to checkout
6. Enter your shipping and billing info
7. Complete payment

<Callout kind="info">
All orders provide certificates of analysis (COA) and tracking once shipped.
</Callout>
