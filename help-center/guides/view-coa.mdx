## Get Your COA

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate lot number">
    Find the lot number on your product label or confirmation email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to COA portal">
    Visit `https://claritylabsusa.com/coa`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter lot number">
    Input your lot number in the search field and submit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download COA">
    View or download the PDF certificate for your records.
  </Step>
</Steps>
